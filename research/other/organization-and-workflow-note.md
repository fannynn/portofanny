# Organization And Workflow Note

## Current Structure

- `research/sources.md` is the main index of the 10 selected experts
- `research/linkedin-posts/` contains recent LinkedIn post notes grouped by author
- `research/youtube-transcripts/` contains transcript-first files grouped by video
- `research/other/` contains supporting notes on selection logic, workflow, troubleshooting, and repo organization

## Why I Organized It This Way

I wanted the repo to stay usable for a later playbook, not just for this assignment deadline.

That is why I separated:

- the source index
- raw or near-raw collected material
- my own synthesis and workflow notes

The transcript files also include my notes at the end, because I wanted the reasoning to stay close to the raw source instead of scattering it into a separate document.

## Workflow Note

I committed and pushed as the structure improved instead of waiting for one final dump. That helped me keep the files cleaner while I was still collecting material, testing tools, and refining which experts actually belonged in the final set.
